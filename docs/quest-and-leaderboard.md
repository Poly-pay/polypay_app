# Quest & Leaderboard

## Overview

Polypay Quest is a 6-week campaign where users earn points by creating accounts and making transactions. Top performers on the weekly leaderboard receive ZEN token rewards.

**Campaign Details:**
- **Start Date:** February 6, 2026 at 12:00 UTC
- **Duration:** 6 weeks
- **Weekly Reward Pool:** $700 USD (paid in ZEN tokens)

---

## How to Earn Points

| Action | Points | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Account Creation | 100 pts | Awarded to the account creator when the account completes its first successful TRANSFER or BATCH transaction |
| Transaction | 50 pts | Awarded to the transaction initiator for each successful TRANSFER or BATCH transaction |

**Example:** If you create an account and initiate its first transaction, you earn 150 pts (100 + 50).

---

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard tracks user rankings based on total points earned.

**Views:**
- **Weekly:** Rankings reset each week. Only points earned during the current week count.
- **All-Time:** Cumulative points across the entire campaign.

**Top 100** users on the weekly leaderboard are eligible for rewards.

---

## Reward Distribution

Weekly rewards are distributed to the Top 100 users:

| Rank | Percentage | Reward (USD) |
|------|------------|--------------|
| 1st | 10% | $70 |
| 2nd | 7% | $49 |
| 3rd | 5% | $35 |
| 4th - 5th | 4% each | $28 |
| 6th - 20th | 2% each | $14 |
| 21st - 50th | ~0.83% each | ~$5.83 |
| 51st - 100th | 0.3% each | $2.10 |
| > 100th | 0% | $0 |

Rewards are converted to ZEN tokens at the current market price.

---

## How to Claim Rewards

1. **Wait for week to end** - Rewards can only be claimed after the week completes
2. **Go to Leaderboard** - Navigate to the Leaderboard page
3. **Select completed week** - Click on a past week tab
4. **Click Claim** - If you're in Top 100, the Claim button will appear
5. **Confirm transaction** - ZEN tokens will be sent to your wallet

**Note:** A pink dot on a week tab indicates you have unclaimed rewards for that week.

---

## FAQ

**Q: When does each week start and end?**
A: Each week runs for 7 days starting from the campaign start date (February 6, 2026 at 12:00 UTC).

**Q: Can I claim rewards from multiple weeks at once?**
A: No, you must claim each week's rewards separately.

**Q: What happens if I don't claim my rewards?**
A: Unclaimed rewards remain available. There is no expiration.

**Q: How is my rank calculated?**
A: Rank is based on total points earned during that week. Higher points = higher rank.
